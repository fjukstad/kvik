Welcome!

Kvik is an interactive system for exploring the dynamics of carcinogenesis through integrated studies of biological pathways and genomic data. It provides researchers with a lightweight web application for navigating through biological pathways from the KEGG database integrated with genomic data from the Norwegian Women and Cancer (NOWAC) postgenome biobank.
